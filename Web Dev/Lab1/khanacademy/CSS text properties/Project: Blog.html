About me

I am a programmer and an iOS developer. I started coding as a hobby and soon realized that it was my passion. I decided to pursue a career in software development and now work as an iOS developer.

How I became an iOS Developer

I started learning how to code by taking online courses and working on personal projects. I then pursued a degree in computer science and took courses in iOS development. I gained practical experience by working on iOS projects as part of my studies and internships. After graduation, I landed my first job as an iOS developer and have been continuing to learn and improve my skills ever since.
